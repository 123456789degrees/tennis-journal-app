MatchMind was built using the **UA Framework**, a three-stage process for going from a raw idea to something you can actually build with confidence:

1. **Discovery** — figure out who this is for, what problem it really solves, and pressure-test that against real people before writing a line of code.
2. **Analysis** — study the landscape, figure out who else has a stake in the product, and turn the problem into concrete jobs-to-be-done and scenarios.
3. **Specification** — turn the analysis into an actual screen-by-screen spec and clickable wireframes, then walk every scenario through them to check nothing breaks.
