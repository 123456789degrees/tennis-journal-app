# MatchBook Interview — Phone Cheat Sheet

**Golden rules:** Ask "when's the last time…", never "would you…". Don't pitch in part 1. Let silences sit.

---

## Part 1 — Just get them talking (NO app talk)

**Playing someone again**
- Last time you played someone you'd played before — tell me about it.
- How often do you face the same people in a season?
- Did you remember their game, or figure it out again?

**A tough match**
- A match you felt you should've won but didn't — what happened?
- Ever known an opponent's weakness but couldn't use it?

**Getting ready**
- Last match vs someone specific — what did you do to prep?
- Do you ever ask anyone how an opponent plays?

**Recording / remembering**
- After a match, do you write anything down? Last time?
- If not — what happens to all that info?

**Tools**
- Ever used an app or notebook for this? What happened?

---

## Part 2 — Only AFTER they describe a real problem

Show the idea:
1. "Type an opponent's name → see their playstyle + a plan to beat them."
2. "And it auto-looks at your recent matches and tells you what to practice."

**Listen for (most important):**
- Do they believe the AI tips would actually be good?
- Or do they say "my coach already tells me that" / "wouldn't trust an app"?

Prompts:
- Show me how you'd actually use this.
- What would make you trust — or not trust — it?
- When would you actually open this?

---

## Close
- Thanks!
- Anyone else I should talk to?
- Okay to come back with a follow-up?
